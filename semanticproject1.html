facts about books

Books are fascinating objects with a rich history. There are around 130 million published books, with the Bible being the most sold. The largest book in the world, according to Guinness World Records, is "This the Prophet Mohamed," measuring 16.40 feet x 26.44 feet.

The oldest book in the world is the "Diamond Sutra," dating back to 868 AD.
